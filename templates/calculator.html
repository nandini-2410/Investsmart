SIP Calculator – Systematic Investment Plan Calculator

A SIP calculator is a tool that helps determine the returns on investments in mutual funds. SIPs allow investing fixed sums at regular intervals.

How SIP Calculator Works?

It calculates the maturity amount based on the formula:
M = P × ({[1 + i]^n – 1} / i) × (1 + i)

For example, investing Rs. 1,000 per month for 12 months at 12% annual interest results in approximately Rs. 12,809.

Benefits of SIP Calculator

  • Helps determine the amount to invest.
  • Shows total investment value.
  • Provides estimated returns.

Lump Calculator

A lump investment is when the depositor invests a significant sum of money in a particular mutual fund scheme.

How Lump Calculator Works?

The formula is as follows:

A = P (1 + r/n) ^ nt

For example, imagine investing Rs. 15 Lakh in a fund with a 12% return for a 5-year period compounding every 6 months, resulting in approximately Rs. 26,43,513.

Benefits of SIP Calculator

  • Provides close return estimates despite market risks, helping in better financial planning.
  • An online tool accessible anytime, eliminating manual effort and saving time.
  • Ensures accurate calculations, reducing errors in investment planning.

SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) Calculator

Under SWP, if you invest a lump sum in a mutual fund, you can set an amount you’ll withdraw regularly and the frequency at which you’ll withdraw.

How SWP Calculator Works?

Here, an individual has invested Rs. 50,000 for a tenure of 1 year along with a systematic withdrawal of Rs. 1,000 per month. The interest rate stands at 10%.

As such, the total return of investments after the end of the tenure stands at Rs. 4,565.

Benefits of SWP Calculator

  • An SWP provides a regular monthly income while generating returns on the total investment.
  • No expertise is required—just enter the details, and the calculator provides instant results.
  • Available online, allowing users to plan withdrawals anytime, anywhere.
